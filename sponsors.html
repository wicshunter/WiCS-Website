Corporate Sponsorship

Our Sponsors

WiCS are able to do what we do with the help of our generous sponsors.

We hosts a number of academic and social events throughout the school year, including tehcnical workshops, hackathons, and panels.

As a corporate sponsor of WiCS, you can help us achieve our goals by directly providing support and mentorship to our community, hosting virtual events for students, and more!

Technical Skills

We organize many technical events to provide opportunities for our members to build their skills and learn different fields in tech.

Through our Tech Events, we allow our members to work on their soft and hard skills, which will eventually, help them land internships in the industry/field that they want.

Networking

We organize a lot of social events to provide networking opportunities for our members.

By creating this space, we are allowing our members to socialize and learn from not only one another, but also professionals from industries and fields that our members are interested in.

On Site Visit

We organize on site visits at offices of the best companies in Manhattan to provide a learning experience of the company.

Through this expereince, members will have the opportunity to netowkr with professionals, learn about a field/company, and help determine if it is the right fit for them.

Job Positions

We provide our members with resources and lists of internship/job opportunities available.

Through our biweekly resource newsletters and email blasts, members get informed of different types of opportunities. Resources include mentorships, workshops, panels, and outside events.
